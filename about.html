dean wallflower

is a media artist specialising in
graphics and visual effects.

this can be anything from photography, produced video, real-time visuals, motion graphics, illustration, and more recently video games.

he has produced portrait photographs for album covers, music videos, video installations, audio-reactive visuals for both live-streams and for musical-performances, and more.

it is my goal to learn programming in order
to combine these skills into a more concise product.
